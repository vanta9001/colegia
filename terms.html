Jujutsu Kaisen is a dark fantasy series that blends supernatural horror, martial-arts-inspired combat, and tragedy.

Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, who becomes the vessel of the King of Curses, Sukuna.

It introduces cursed spirits, sorcerers, and dangerous battles where survival is never guaranteed.

Through his journey, Yuji, Megumi, Nobara, and Gojo fight to protect humanity from curses while facing devastating losses.

It explores themes of life, death, and the value of human connection. Every victory comes with sacrifice and consequence.

Jujutsu Kaisen captures the struggle of sorcerers in a world torn between light and darkness.
